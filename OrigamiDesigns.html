Some Facts About Animals

Panda

Facts About Panda

  • An adult can eat 12-38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  • A panda Can Poop 28 Kilos per day!
  • Pandas have so many fans because they look cute

Tedddy

Facts About Teddy

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902!
  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906!
  • Washable teddy bears didn't appear until 1954 when the stuffing was switched to an easier to clean nylon fill.Before that, they were stuffed with mohair, wool, and other hard to clean fills.

Pigeon

Facts About Pigeon

  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities!
  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.!
  • They might be the first domesticated bird!

Camel

Facts About Camel

  • Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!
  • Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day!
  • Don't make a camel angry - they can spit as a way to distract whatever they think is a threat!

Cicada

Facts About Cicada

  • Most have red-orange eyes. But occasionally cicadas have blue, white or grayish eyes. Keep on the lookout!
  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs!
  • Their short adult life is not unusual for bugs!

Chameleon

Facts About Chameleon

  • Their feet work like salad tongs!
  • Chameleon eyes have a 360-degree arc of vision and can see two directions at once!
  • Chameleons can't hear much!
